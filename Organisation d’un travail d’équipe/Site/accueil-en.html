Discover the splendor of the Palace of Versailles, an iconic residence of the French kings such as Louis XIV, Louis XV, and Louis XVI. A symbol of grandeur and prestige, this architectural gem from the 17th century will immerse you in the fascinating history of the French monarchy. Visit the sumptuous rooms where these sovereigns lived and decided the fate of the country, and stroll through the magnificent French gardens. An unforgettable experience awaits you at the heart of this national treasure.
